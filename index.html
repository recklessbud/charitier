Welcome to Charitier

Welcome to Charitier, a loving home for orphaned children. We are dedicated to providing a nurturing and supportive environment where every child feels safe, valued, and loved. Our mission is to give these children a chance at a brighter future, where they can grow, learn, and thrive.

About Us

At Charitier

At Charitier, we prioritize the well-being and holistic development of each child in our care.

  • Our mission is to provide comprehensive care that encompasses the physical, emotional, educational, and social needs of our children
  • Education is a cornerstone of empowerment, and we are dedicated to offering our children opportunities to learn and grow academically.
  • We firmly believe in the power of community and partnerships. We actively collaborate with local organizations, volunteers, and supporters who share our vision of creating a better world for orphaned children.

We understand the importance of education in shaping a child's future. That's why we provide comprehensive schooling programs that foster intellectual growth and empower our children to become knowledgeable, confident individuals. Additionally, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and life skills training, allowing our children to explore their talents and develop well-rounded personalities.

Residential Care

We provide a safe, secure, and nurturing residential environment where orphaned children can find a loving home. Our dedicated caregivers offer round-the-clock support, ensuring that each child's physical and emotional needs are met.

Education Programs

We offer comprehensive educational programs that aim to empower our children academically. Our qualified teachers provide a quality education, tailored to each child's individual needs, helping them excel in their studies and expand their knowledge.

Healthcare and Medical Support

The well-being of our children is of utmost importance. We provide access to regular health check-ups, medical care, and necessary treatments. Our dedicated healthcare staff ensures that each child receives proper healthcare support and guidance.

Nutritious Meals

We believe that proper nutrition is vital for the healthy development of our children. We provide balanced and nutritious meals, catering to their dietary needs, and fostering their growth and well-being

Recreational Activities

We encourage our children to engage in various recreational activities, including sports, arts and crafts, music, and cultural events. These activities promote their physical fitness, creative expression, and social interaction, allowing them to explore their interests and talents.
